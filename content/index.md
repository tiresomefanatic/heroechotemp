## What is the Foundation?

<div class="concept-visual">
  <img src="/images/foundation-concept.svg" alt="Foundation concept visualization" />
</div>

Built for today's design needs our foundation sets concrete standards for all aspects of the design process. We aim to create a cohesive experience across all platforms while maintaining flexibility for unique implementations.

## Goals

### Cohesive Experience

Creating a seamless experience across all digital and physical touchpoints.

### Accessibility

Ensuring our designs are accessible to all users across various platforms.

### Simplicity and contribution

Making decisions simple while design remains highly customized.
